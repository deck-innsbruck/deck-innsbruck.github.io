## Who can join?

DECK is mainly aimed at students from all semesters as well as employees of the University of Innsbruck. Everyone interested in strategic card games, pen-and-paper role-playing games, tabletop formats, or similar game systems is welcome.

You do not need to be an expert and you do not need tournament experience. Many of our formats are suitable for beginners.

## What you do not need

You do not need your own collection, miniatures, a finished deck, or prior experience to join DECK. For many formats, materials can be shared, explained, or prepared together.

If you are unsure whether an event is suitable for beginners, you can contact us at any time.

## What you can bring

It is helpful if you are interested in one or more of our formats, for example:

- strategic card games
- trading card games
- deckbuilding games
- pen-and-paper role-playing games
- Warhammer 40,000
- Kill Team
- other tabletop or skirmish systems
- board games with a strategic focus

New game suggestions are also welcome. DECK should continue to grow with the interests of the community.

## Getting started as a new player

We want to make it as easy as possible to get started with new game systems. Questions are explicitly welcome. Experienced players are happy to help with rules, decks, armies, characters, or first game sessions.

Our goal is to create an open atmosphere where people can try things out, learn, and improve together.

## Helping out

DECK lives from members contributing their own interests and skills. For example, you can help with:

- organizing game nights
- setting up and supporting events
- explaining rules to new players
- running pen-and-paper sessions
- planning tournaments
- supporting social media or website content
- science communication around games, strategy, and design

If you would like to help, just contact us.
