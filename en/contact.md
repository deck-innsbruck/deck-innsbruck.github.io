## What you can contact us about

Feel free to contact us if you:

- want to join DECK
- have questions about our game nights or events
- want to suggest a specific game or format
- want to organize a pen-and-paper session, tabletop format, or tournament
- are interested in cooperating with DECK
- are unsure whether you can participate without prior experience

New players are explicitly welcome. You do not need to own your own material or have tournament experience to join us.
