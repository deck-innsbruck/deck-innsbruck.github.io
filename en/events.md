## What happens at our events?

Depending on the event, different formats may be the focus. Typical activities include:

- open play sessions
- discovering new games
- introductions to rules and game systems
- casual games
- smaller tournament formats
- pen-and-paper sessions
- tabletop and skirmish games
- exchange about strategy, deckbuilding, design, and game culture

You usually do not need prior experience. At many events, materials can be explained, shared, or prepared together.

## Who are the events for?

Our events are especially aimed at students from all semesters as well as employees of the University of Innsbruck. Depending on the event, other interested people are also welcome.

It is important to us that new people can join easily. If you are unsure whether an event is suitable for you, feel free to contact us.

## Collaborations

Would you like to organize an event with DECK or develop an existing format together? We are happy to receive requests from local groups, studios, associations, university units, and other initiatives.

Possible collaborations include game nights, workshops, tournaments, introduction events, or science communication formats around games and strategy.
