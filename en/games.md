## Our games and formats

DECK is open to many different game systems. Some formats are played regularly, while others depend on interest, events, or available players.

If you would like to try a specific game or introduce a format yourself, feel free to contact us.

## Deckbuilders and similar card games

In addition to classic trading card games, we also play deckbuilding games and strategic card games without a collectible card structure.
