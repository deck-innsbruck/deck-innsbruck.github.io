## Welcome to DECK

DECK is a student community for strategic games at the University of Innsbruck.  
We bring together students, university employees, and players interested in trading card games, deckbuilders, pen-and-paper role-playing games, tabletop, and related formats.

New players are welcome — whether you already have experience or want to try a game system for the first time.
