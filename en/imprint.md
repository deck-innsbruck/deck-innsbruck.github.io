## Purpose and editorial focus

DECK is a student association for strategic card games, deckbuilders, pen-and-paper role-playing games, tabletop formats, and science communication around games, strategy, and design.

The purpose of this website is to provide information about DECK, our activities, games, meetings, events, and contact options.
